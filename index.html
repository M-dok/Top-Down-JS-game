How to Play

The goal of the game is to last as long as possible against waves of enemies that increase each round

Movement

To move your character you must press the WASD keys

  • W - to move up
  • A - to move left
  • S - to move down
  • D - to move right

Attcking

To attack enemies use the arrow keys

  • Up Arrow - to shoot up
  • Left Arrow - to shoot left
  • Down Arrow- to shoot down
  • Right Arrow - to shoot right

Power Ups

chests will appear from time to time in the center of the map. Be sure to get them, because they hold power ups that can help you

  • Speed - Increases for a while
  • Bounce - Bullets bounce off the walls
  • Wild Shot - Changes your straight attack to a spread attack
  • Slow Down - Freezes enemies in place
